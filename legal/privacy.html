Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how IG Club LLC (doing business as “Dreams Active”), a Wyoming limited liability company, USA (“Dreams Active”, “we”, “us”), collects, uses, and shares information when you visit dreamsactive.com or purchase and use our digital wellness & education content (the “Service”).

1. Who we are

The Service is operated by IG Club LLC (DBA “Dreams Active”), 1309 Coffeen Avenue STE 1200, Sheridan, WY 82801, USA. For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at info@dreamsactive.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Information you provide: your name and email address when you purchase the course or contact us; messages you send us; content you post in the course community chat.
  • Payment information: payments are processed by third-party payment processors. We do not store your full card number; we receive confirmation of payment and limited billing details (such as the last digits of the card and billing country).
  • Automatically collected information: IP address, device and browser type, pages viewed, referring pages, and approximate location derived from your IP address, collected via cookies and similar technologies.

We do not ask for and do not want to receive medical or health data through the Service. Any information you voluntarily share in the community chat is visible to other participants — please share thoughtfully.

3. How we use information

  • To provide access to the course and community chat;
  • To process payments and send purchase confirmations;
  • To respond to your questions and provide support;
  • To send service messages about your access and important changes;
  • To analyze and improve the Service (aggregated analytics);
  • To prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Use;
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal bases

Where the EU/UK GDPR applies, we process your data to perform our contract with you (providing the course), based on our legitimate interests (improving and securing the Service), with your consent (where required, e.g. non-essential cookies), and to comply with legal obligations.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Service providers that help us run the Service — payment processors, hosting providers, e-mail delivery, analytics, and customer-support tools — under contracts limiting their use of your data;
  • Community platforms used for the course chat (your chosen display name and messages are visible to other members);
  • Authorities where required by law, or to protect our rights, users, or the public;
  • A successor entity in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies needed for the site to function and, with consent where required, analytics cookies to understand how the site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling essential cookies may break parts of the site.

7. Data retention

We keep personal data for as long as needed to provide the Service and for legitimate business or legal purposes (for example, tax and accounting records of purchases). We then delete or anonymize it.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal data, object to or restrict certain processing, and withdraw consent. California residents have rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know, delete, and correct; we do not sell or “share” personal information as defined by the CPRA. To exercise any right, email info@dreamsactive.com. You will not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

9. International transfers

We are based in the United States, and your data is processed in the US and other countries where our providers operate. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) for transfers from the EEA/UK.

10. Children

The Service is intended for adults and is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above shows the latest revision; material changes will be announced on the site or by email.
